Case Study: Data & Digitalization

Digital Transformation: The Ledger That Changed Everything

A look at how a decades-old logistics firm finally escaped the 'paper trap,' achieving 100% data accuracy and massive time savings.

"We knew our records were a mess. Every audit was a month-long nightmare, based on images and handwritten notes."

The Hidden Cost of Chaos

'TransGlobal Logistics' was profitable, but inefficient. Their financial reporting was a collection of manually-entered data from disorganized paper files and thousands of un-validated images. The cost of error correction and the time spent preparing for quarterly audits consumed their entire finance team, preventing them from doing any strategic work.

The Global Financials Solution

We executed a complete digital overhaul, turning their chaotic data into a clean, automated system:

  • **Data Extraction & Cleaning:** Used advanced tools to extract and validate all historical data from images and PDFs (as seen in our featured project).
  • **Cloud Migration:** Transitioned their entire general ledger to a modern, secure cloud accounting platform.
  • **Automation:** Implemented automated rules for expense classification and bank reconciliation, eliminating manual entry errors.

The Result: Time and Accuracy

TransGlobal Logistics achieved **100% financial data accuracy** and reduced the time required for their annual tax preparation from four weeks to three days. Their finance team moved from data entry to strategic analysis, finally giving the company the competitive edge it needed.
