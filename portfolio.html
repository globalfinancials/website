Featured Project

Unstructured Data to Financial Ledger: 100% Accuracy

A case study in transforming unorganized image data into clean, compliant, and ready-to-use financial records.

Project Overview

The client provided a set of unorganized and randomly arranged images containing a full year's cash inflow and outflow data, with each month spread over four photos. I extracted key details including dates, debit/credit descriptions, and balances, and organized them into clear monthly cash ledger accounts.

I then prepared a comprehensive yearly ledger and a concise summary report in Excel. The project was completed ahead of the deadline with 100% accuracy, ensuring the client received well-structured, error-free, and ready-to-use financial records for decision-making and reporting purposes.
