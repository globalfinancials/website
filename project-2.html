Strategic Data Analysis

Visualizing Growth & Profitability for Restora Comfort Pods Ltd.

The Challenge

Restora Comfort Pods (RCPL) operates a standardized hospitality model across Asia, Africa, and Europe. To justify significant capital for expansion, management needed to move beyond basic sales figures. They lacked visibility into which specific pod types (e.g., Solo vs. Executive) and locations drove sustainable profit, occupancy, and customer retention over the last three years.

Our Solution

We consolidated complex datasets covering Revenue, Employee Allocation, and Regional Expenses into a cohesive Power BI ecosystem. We developed 5 interactive dashboards to isolate performance variables, allowing RCPL to drill down from a global view into specific city-level profitability and employee efficiency metrics.

The Insight

The analysis revealed hidden growth corridors and underperforming assets. By visualizing the data, we identified high-occupancy regions ideal for expansion and flagged low-margin facilities for operational restructuring, providing a data-backed foundation for the company's next strategic phase.
