Within 18 months, Acme Retail had shed its most toxic debt and transformed its operating model. They moved from a **net loss of 8% to a strong 15% annual profit margin**. By making necessary, data-backed decisions, they successfully pivoted from a dying legacy brand into a lean, profitable enterprise.
