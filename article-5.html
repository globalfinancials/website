Case Study: Cost Analysis & Pricing

The Coffee Shop That Outsmarted Inflation

How one beloved local business navigated surging costs by strategically adjusting their pricing and sourcing, boosting margins without raising a single complaint.

"We were terrified to raise prices and lose our loyal customers. Our accountant showed us the profit margin on every single cup."

The Erosion of Profit

'The Daily Grind' was a community cornerstone, but when global coffee, milk, and energy prices soared, their already thin profit margins began to evaporate. The owners knew they had to act, but feared a blanket price increase would alienate their dedicated customer base and drive them to competitors. They needed surgical precision, not a blunt instrument.

The Global Financials Solution

We implemented a detailed **Unit Cost and Contribution Margin Analysis** for their top 50 menu items. This revealed surprising truths about which drinks were carrying the most profit:

  • **Targeted Pricing:** Raised prices slightly (less than \$0.50) on 5 items with the highest demand and lowest visibility.
  • **Cost Engineering:** Found a new, equally high-quality milk supplier, reducing dairy costs by 15%.
  • **Menu Optimization:** Used the data to promote the highest-margin items through strategic placement on the menu board.

The Result: Margin Growth

The Daily Grind successfully absorbed the rising inflation. Their average **transaction margin increased by 7%**, achieved almost entirely through cost engineering and targeted pricing, proving that intelligent financial strategy can protect both your profits and your loyal customers.
